Portfolio

"The Pursuit of Excellence" is a gripping 10-minute documentary/drama about Sandun's journey through good and tough times in school. Sandun works hard alone, giving up time with friends and trips to study a lot. The film shows how Sandun is focused, while his friends, who work together, celebrate their successes.

When Sandun sees his results on the board, it's not what he expected. The movie reveals the emotional toll of his sacrifices and the difference between his efforts and his friends' success. It makes the viewers think about the challenges of trying to be the best in school and the advantages of working in teams.
